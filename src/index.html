Let’s take a look at Lenoir’s features in more detail:

User Interface

In this modern world, we all spend the majority of our time scrolling through the same handful of apps; Facebook, Twitter, TikTok, Whatsapp, YouTube, Reddit etc.

Even when we’re browsing the internet, perhaps doing actual work, a combination of these apps will be open at all times. These apps are important to us as this is how we organise our lives, consume information and maintain relationships. Therefore, your web browser should logically integrate them into the browsing experience.

AppStore

Apps have come to play an immense part in the way we live our lives today. Via them, we can navigate almost the entire western corpus of music (and even more than that), find romantic partners, and handle our diaries, documentation, and payments. Hence, Lenoir browser provides you with an easy access to download and install any type of App and games swiftly.

However, the interesting part is that whether you install a game or App, it will be stored on the built-in space made available on this browser

No Address Bar and no Tabs

Most times when we're surfing the internet, maybe doing some homework, a mixture of these applications would be available at all times while using this browser. As apps are significant to us, this is how we manage our lives, ingest knowledge and sustain relationships, so they will be sync into your browsing experience.

When navigating to these websites, you don’t want to use the URL bar, you want to click on the app like you would on your phone. This is why Lenoir removed the address bar and replaced it with web browser specific apps and a unique app store to download more.

Multi-tasking

In today’s busy societies, there is no choice but to multitask. Have an effective multitasking tool and strategy is essential to succeed.

Lenoir has removed the unnecessary address bar and replaced it with multitasking apps. Users can now listen to songs in the radio app while searching shazam for the name of their new favourite songs at the same time. They could even search for it on YouTube while simultaneously messaging their friends on Whatsapp and Messenger about it.

This level of intuitive multitasking has only been available to mobile users, until now.

However you want to multitask, Lenoir lets you do it.

Ad Blocking

At Lenoir, users pay the monthly fee to be protected online. We value our users because they are our clients, and we want to do everything to make sure they are protected online. This is why our web browser has built-in ad blocking, and not just any ad blocking software, premium ad blocking software. This ensures that you can browse to your heart’s content on Lenoir with no worries of seeing annoying ads or being tracked.

Data Security

We know your data and personal information is important to you, which is why Lenoir has been designed to protect this from both large and small corporations as well as online criminals.

We ensure this is all taken care of in the background so you can browse the internet carefree, enjoying a modern and fun experience while knowing you are fully protected.

Vienna

Thanks to the ongoing pandemic and the tiresome lockdown many are still enduring, travelling continues to not be possible. Because of this, we decided to name each version of Lenoir Browser after a beautiful city. The first version will be named after Vienna, the capital of Austria, and inside the browser, users will find themed wallpapers and a special app about Vienna with a virtual tour. But that’s not all! Users from Vienna will have access to the browser for free, and any following version will also grant free use of Lenoir to their residents.
